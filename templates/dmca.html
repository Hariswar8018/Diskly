DMCA Policy

Copyright infringement notification and takedown procedure.

1. Introduction

Diskly respects the intellectual property rights of others. We comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) and respond to properly submitted copyright infringement notices.

2. Reporting Copyright Infringement

If you believe your content has been used without authorization, you may submit a DMCA takedown request.

Your notice must include:

  • Your full name and contact information
  • Description of the copyrighted work
  • URL of the infringing content
  • A statement of good faith belief
  • A statement under penalty of perjury
  • Your physical or electronic signature

3. Takedown Procedure

Upon receiving a valid DMCA request, Diskly will:

  • Remove or disable access to the content
  • Notify the user who uploaded it
  • Take action for repeat violations

4. Counter Notification

If you believe your content was removed by mistake, you may submit a counter-notification including:

  • Your contact details
  • Identification of removed content
  • A statement under penalty of perjury
  • Consent to jurisdiction

5. Repeat Infringers

Accounts that repeatedly violate copyright laws may be permanently suspended.
